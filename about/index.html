About Pastor Micah Y Sarki

I am Pastor Micah Yohanna Sarki, dedicated to sharing God's love and truth through powerful preaching, inspiring books, and authentic community fellowship.

Pastor Micah Yohanna Sarki is a devoted servant of God with roots in Kano State. He has been called to lead and prepare individuals to fulfill their divine purpose and to guide God's people through sound biblical teachings and intentional youth mentoring programs.

With over a decade of experience as a clergy member, he is dedicated to bringing revival to communities. He is a seasoned speaker, ministering across Nigeria, and also serves as a missionary in the Niger Republic, driven by a strong desire to reach the Sahara nations.

Ministry Journey

Pastor Micah Yohanna Sarki's journey into ministry began at a young age when he felt the calling of God upon his life. Born and raised in Nigeria, he witnessed firsthand the transformative power of the Gospel in his community and family.

After completing his theological education, Pastor Micah began serving in various capacities within the church. His passion for teaching and preaching quickly became evident as he demonstrated a unique ability to make complex biblical truths accessible to people from all walks of life.

Throughout his ministry, Pastor Micah has been committed to three core principles: authentic biblical teaching, compassionate pastoral care, and practical application of God's word in daily life. These principles have guided his approach to ministry and have been instrumental in the growth and development of the congregations he has served.

Today, Pastor Micah continues to serve with the same passion and dedication that marked the beginning of his ministry. Through his preaching, writing, and pastoral care, he remains committed to helping people discover and fulfill God's purpose for their lives.

Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ with clarity and compassion, equipping believers for spiritual growth and empowering them to make a positive impact in their communities. We are committed to teaching God's word with integrity and helping people discover their divine purpose.

Vision

To see a generation of believers who are deeply rooted in God's word, passionately committed to His kingdom, and actively engaged in transforming their world through love, service, and the power of the Gospel. We envision communities touched by the love of Christ and lives transformed by His grace.

Core Values

Love

I believe that love is the foundation of all ministry. Every action i take is motivated by God's love for me and my love for others.

Integrity

I am committed to honesty, transparency, and authenticity in all my dealings, ensuring that my life reflects the character of Christ.

Excellence

I strive for excellence in everything i do, believing that God deserves my very best in service, worship, and ministry.
